Best of Calabria reaches Polish and English-speaking travellers interested in discovering Calabria authentically. If you run a tourism-related business in the region or want to jointly promote Calabria – we would love to discuss collaboration opportunities.

Local businesses and service providers. We work with hotels, agriturismi, restaurants, guides, and tour organisers across Calabria. We offer honest, editorial coverage – we never publish sponsored content without clear disclosure. If your property or service truly stands out in the region, we are happy to feature it and recommend it to our readers.

We publish in both Polish and English, reaching travellers from Poland as well as English-speaking visitors planning a trip to Calabria. Our audience consists mainly of independent travellers interested in culture, cuisine and lesser-known parts of the region. If we have up-to-date reach statistics, we are happy to share them individually when discussing a collaboration.

Collaboration formats may include a labelled sponsored article, an editorial review, a directory listing, a display campaign, newsletter, social media or a press trip. We prefer to work with brands and services that operate in Calabria and are genuinely useful to travellers – from accommodation and dining to local transport, cooking workshops, regional producers and outdoor activity organisers.

Travel agencies and tour operators. If you organise trips to Calabria or plan to add the region to your portfolio, we can collaborate on materials that help your clients get to know the destination better. Our content can serve as a source of inspiration and practical information for travellers.

Tourism boards and local authorities. We partner with regional and local tourism organisations to promote lesser-known parts of Calabria on the Polish and international market. We believe Calabria deserves far more attention – and we are glad to help build it.

Interested? Write to us at contact@bestofcalabria.com with a brief description of your business and your collaboration idea. We reply to every message.

You can contact us in Polish, English or Italian. We usually respond within 2–5 working days. In your first email, it helps to include your company name, location in Calabria, website or Instagram, scope of activity, proposed collaboration timeframe, and budget or expectations.

We are open to various collaboration models – barter, affiliate and paid campaigns. Regardless of format, what matters to us is quality: a business connected to Calabria, real value for travellers, original photos and up-to-date contact details. If this sounds like a good fit, write to contact@bestofcalabria.com.
