Best of Calabria reaches Polish and English-speaking travellers interested in discovering Calabria authentically. If you run a tourism-related business in the region or want to jointly promote Calabria – we would love to discuss collaboration opportunities.

Local businesses and service providers. We work with hotels, agriturismi, restaurants, guides, and tour organisers across Calabria. We offer honest, editorial coverage – we never publish sponsored content without clear disclosure. If your property or service truly stands out in the region, we are happy to feature it and recommend it to our readers.

Travel agencies and tour operators. If you organise trips to Calabria or plan to add the region to your portfolio, we can collaborate on materials that help your clients get to know the destination better. Our content can serve as a source of inspiration and practical information for travellers.

Tourism boards and local authorities. We partner with regional and local tourism organisations to promote lesser-known parts of Calabria on the Polish and international market. We believe Calabria deserves far more attention – and we are glad to help build it.

Interested? Write to us at contact@bestofcalabria.com with a brief description of your business and your collaboration idea. We reply to every message.
