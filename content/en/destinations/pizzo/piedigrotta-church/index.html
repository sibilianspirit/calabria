--- slug: piedigrotta-church title: Piedigrotta Church translationKey: destinations/pizzo/piedigrotta-church wp_id: 2044 ---

A church in a tufa cave whose history dates back to the 17th century, when shipwrecked sailors placed a painting of the Madonna here. The life-size stone figures depicting biblical scenes were carved from around 1880 by local artists Angelo Barone and his son Alfonso Barone. One of Calabria's most unique and mysterious sites.
