The History of Terme di Antonimina – Ancient Roots and Traditions

Terme di Antonimina, known by the name Acque Sante, meaning Holy Waters, is one of the important thermal sites in this part of Calabria. Antonimina lies in the Locride area, inland from the Ionian coast, which is why many travellers associate this spa with Locri – the nearest larger town and natural point of access. The historical background of the region is relevant here: ancient Locri Epizephyrii was one of the important cities of Magna Graecia on the Ionian coast of Calabria.

Relaxation and Regeneration at the Highest Level – Spa Treatments in Calabria

Calabria offers various forms of relaxation involving thermal water, but in the case of Antonimina it is best to stick to what can be described without marketing additions. The focus is usually on classic balneological treatments and procedures associated with spa therapy, rather than eye-catching names for wellness packages. This makes it easier to distinguish the real health offering from general regional promotion.

In practice, it is worth remembering the basic organisational rules. In Italian thermal baths, SSN-funded treatment cycles often comprise 12 sessions over 12 days, but the number and type of services depend on the indication and the facility's accreditation. Medical qualification is usually required, which allows the appropriate type of treatment to be selected.

If you are using the baths privately, prices and opening hours are best checked directly in the facility's official price list. Such data change seasonally, so without an update date it is not worth basing travel plans on individual figures found online. This is particularly important if you want to combine a health stay with several days of exploring Locride.

The trip itself is well planned so that the baths form part of a wider day in the area. A short drive separates Antonimina from Gerace, one of the best-preserved medieval towns in Calabria with its Norman cathedral. Nearby also lies the Locri Epizephyrii archaeological park and the Costa dei Gelsomini coastline on the Ionian Sea, making it easy to arrange a half-day or full-day plan combining relaxation with discovering the region.

Journey to Terme di Antonimina: How to Reach This Calabrian Spa

Terme di Antonimina is best treated as a day-trip destination from Locri or Gerace, rather than as a place cut off from the rest of the region. The most convenient option for tourists remains renting a car, especially if you want to combine the baths with a tour of Locride and the Jasmine Coast. The route from Locri to Antonimina is roughly 10–15 km, depending on the road chosen.

Those preferring public transport can use the Taranto – Reggio Calabria railway connections. The nearest station used by tourists is Locri, and regional trains from Reggio Calabria typically take about 1 hour 20 minutes to 1 hour 40 minutes. From Locri itself to Antonimina is roughly 15–20 minutes by car.

Buses to smaller towns in this part of Calabria do exist, but timetables are worth checking locally. Services can be limited, seasonal and less convenient outside morning hours, so for a short stay a taxi remains a sensible alternative. The good news is that Gerace is just a few kilometres from Locri, making it easy to combine all three places during a single excursion.

Regardless of your mode of transport, a visit to Terme di Antonimina fits well into the relaxed rhythm of exploring this part of Calabria. In the morning you can visit Gerace, then stop at the baths, and at the end of the day return to the Ionian coast of the Costa dei Gelsomini. It is one of those plans that best show the diversity of Locride – from medieval hilltops to seaside landscapes.
